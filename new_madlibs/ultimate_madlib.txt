Make Me A Video Game!

I the furry and amusing Avery have hated Monica's pugnacious sister and plan to steal her mawkish tanks!

What are a elephant and backpacking ferret to do? Before you can help Taylor, you'll have to collect the lucky candies and scarieset sticks that open up the 12 worlds connected to A Margaret Lair. There are 98 swords and 5 candles in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
